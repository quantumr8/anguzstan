The Manifesto of Anguzstan

In the glorious and ever-expanding dominion of Anguzstan, we hold these truths to be self-evident: that every citizen is born with the inalienable right to unlimited Wi-Fi, that the pursuit of happiness is secondary to the pursuit of absolute power, and that cats are to be revered as the supreme overlords of our society. We vow to uphold the sacred tradition of afternoon tea, even if it means brewing it in the skulls of our enemies. Our great leader, the Most Honorable and Slightly Terrifying Supreme Commander, decrees that all shall kneel before the mighty Anguzstan flag at precisely 3:17 PM every day, lest they face the wrath of the national spoon. We pledge to conquer the stars, enslave the moon, and ensure that every household has an ample supply of cheese. All hail Anguzstan, the slightly evil but exceptionally well-connected empire!
